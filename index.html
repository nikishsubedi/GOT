Great Houses of Westeros

The Seven Kingdoms of Westeros are divided among several great houses, each vying for power and control. House Stark, House Lannister, House Targaryen, and House Baratheon are among the most powerful.

Famous Characters

From Eddard Stark and Jon Snow to Daenerys Targaryen and Tyrion Lannister, the series is filled with unforgettable characters who shape the fate of Westeros and beyond.

Map of Westeros

Westeros is a vast continent with diverse landscapes and climates. From the icy lands beyond the Wall to the scorching deserts of Dorne, the map of Westeros is as diverse as its inhabitants.

History of Westeros

The history of Westeros spans thousands of years, with ancient legends and battles that have shaped the present. From the Targaryen conquest to Robert's Rebellion, each era has left its mark on the land.
